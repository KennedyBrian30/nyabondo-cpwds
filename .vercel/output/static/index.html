About Nyabondo Centre for the Disabled

Six Decades of Healing, Hope and Human Dignity

1963 Year Founded

Nyabondo Centre for Persons with Disabilities was established in 1963 by the Medical Mission Sisters, responding to a pressing need for specialised rehabilitation care in western Kenya. What began as a small orthopaedic unit in Sondu has grown into one of Kenya's foremost centres of comprehensive rehabilitation, touching the lives of tens of thousands of persons with physical, sensory, and developmental disabilities.

Located in Kisumu County, Nyabondo offers a continuum of care spanning community outreach and early identification, corrective surgery, physiotherapy, the provision of prosthetics and orthotics, and vocational training that enables beneficiaries to live with independence and purpose. Rooted in values of integrity, respect, and spirituality, the Centre holds the conviction that every person, regardless of ability, holds inherent worth and deserves the opportunity to thrive.

Our Services

Comprehensive Rehabilitation Services

A holistic continuum of care designed to restore function, build confidence, and enable lasting independence for every person we serve.

Community Outreaches

We take services directly to the community, conducting early identification, awareness campaigns, clinical assessments, and registration of persons with disabilities in remote and underserved areas.

Learn More

Corrective Surgeries

Skilled orthopaedic and reconstructive procedures including club foot correction, cleft lip and palate repair, post-burn contracture release, and bone deformity correction restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Learn More

Therapy Services

Individualised physiotherapy and occupational therapy programmes for children and adults, focused on restoring movement, strength, and functional independence across all stages of recovery.

Learn More

Assistive Devices

Custom-made prosthetics, orthotics, walking aids, and mobility equipment crafted using appropriate technology, ensuring every device is functional, affordable, and tailored to the individual's needs.

Learn More

Vocational Training

Practical livelihood skills in dressmaking, weaving, hairdressing, shoemaking, basket weaving, and computer literacy, equipping beneficiaries with the tools to earn, contribute, and thrive independently.

Learn More

Long-term Rehabilitation

Specialised, programme-based therapy for children and adults with cerebral palsy and complex long-term care needs, providing consistent, structured support for measurable progress over time.

Learn More

Inpatient Services

Residential care for patients requiring surgery, intensive rehabilitation, or post-operative recovery. Our inpatient ward provides a safe, supportive environment with round-the-clock clinical attention and holistic care.

Learn More

Outpatient Services

Clinical consultations, follow-up care, therapy sessions, and device fittings for patients who do not require admission. Our outpatient programme ensures continuity of care for hundreds of clients each month.

Learn More

Pre-school Education

An inclusive early childhood programme for children with disabilities, combining stimulation therapy, school-readiness activities, and parental guidance to give every child the strongest possible foundation for learning.

Who We Are

Our Mission, Vision and Values

The purpose, aspiration, and principles that guide every decision, every interaction, and every life we touch at Nyabondo Centre.

Our Mission

To provide quality rehabilitation and medical services, through corrective surgery, therapy, and assistive devices, to persons with disabilities in a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment.

Our Vision

To be a referral centre of excellence in the provision of integral, comprehensive rehabilitation services that are affordable and accessible to all persons with disabilities in Kenya.

Integrity Honesty and transparency in all we do.
Spirituality Work consecrated to God, in service of others.
Quality Exceeding expectations through excellence.
Innovation Creativity and continuous improvement.
Inclusivity Full participation for persons with disabilities.
Responsiveness Attentive to the needs of those we serve.
Accountability Transparent to partners, members, and the public.
Respect Honouring the dignity of every human person.
